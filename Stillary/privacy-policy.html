Privacy Policy

Stillary Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026

Stillary is a local-first photo memory app. Your photos, memo text, and exports stay on your device while you edit. Stillary does, however, use Firebase telemetry in release builds for anonymous usage analytics and crash diagnostics.

1. Photo content stays on device

Stillary does not upload your photos, videos, memo text, month posters, or exported images to Stillary servers. Photo processing, palette extraction, layout rendering, and draft editing all happen locally on your device.

Stillary only writes an image out when you explicitly choose Save or Share. If you share an export, Android and the destination app handle that transfer.

2. Technical data we collect

  • Anonymous usage analytics: When the in-app Anonymous analytics setting is enabled, Stillary sends basic app interaction data such as screen views to Google Firebase Analytics. Photo content and memo text are not included in these events.
  • Crash logs and diagnostics: Release builds send crash and stability diagnostics to Google Firebase Crashlytics so we can investigate failures and improve reliability.
  • Device or app instance identifiers: Firebase services may process app instance or device identifiers required to provide analytics and crash diagnostics.

3. How we use technical data

  • To understand screen flow and feature usage.
  • To diagnose crashes, stability issues, and regressions.
  • To prioritize fixes and product improvements.

4. Third-party services

Stillary currently uses the following Google Firebase services as processors acting on our behalf:

  • Firebase Analytics for anonymous screen-flow telemetry.
  • Firebase Crashlytics for crash reports and diagnostics.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your data for advertising.

5. Your choices

You can turn off Anonymous analytics at any time in Stillary Settings. This stops screen-flow analytics from being sent going forward.

Crash diagnostics are currently kept enabled in release builds for reliability monitoring.

6. Security

Telemetry transmitted to Firebase is encrypted in transit. Photos and draft content remain local unless you explicitly export or share them.
